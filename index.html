Personalized Electronic Reader for Electrochemical Transducers -- PERfECT
PERfECT is the world’s smallest, coin-sized readout unit for remote and wireless organic electrochemical transducer (OECT)  characterization, which is verified that can measure the transfer, output, hysteresis, and transient behaviors of OECT. Organic electrochemical transducer (OECT) is regarded as the next-generation biosensing technology because of its ultrahigh sensitivity, water-stability, and cost-effectiveness.
This work paves the way for developing OECT-based medical devices for truly wearable healthcare monitoring applications.
